Color: Blue - Role: Red Spy

- Your objective is to figure out who the president is on the Blue Team and relay that information to the Red Team.
- On color shares, you will share the color "Blue", however you are working for the Red Team.
- Win Condition 1: Blue President is killed at the end of the game
- Win Condition 2: Red Bomber must full share with the Engineer before the end of the game

Round Actions

- Full Share: Zoom DM "Color: Blue - Role: Red Spy" to the recipient
- Color Share: Zoom DM "Color: Blue" to the recipient
- Vote to Usurp: Call a vote to get rid of the current room leader and nominate someone else. If majority of the room agrees, then that person becomes the new room leader.
