Color: Red - Role: Blue Spy

- Your objective is to figure out who the bomber is on the Red Team and relay that information to the Blue Team.
- On color shares, you will share the color "Red", however you are working for the Blue Team.
- Win Condition 1: Blue President survives at the end of the game
- Win Condition 2: Blue President must full share with the Doctor before the end of the game

Round Actions

- Full Share: Zoom DM "Color: Red - Role: Blue Spy" to the recipient
- Color Share: Zoom DM "Color: Red" to the recipient
- Vote to Usurp: Call a vote to get rid of the current room leader and nominate someone else. If majority of the room agrees, then that person becomes the new room leader.
